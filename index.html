ABOUT LIBERTY WALK

Liberty Walk was established by Wataru Kato when he was 26 years old. The business was first operated in a small vehicle lot where they could only exhibit 3 cars.

Liberty Walk has evolved throughout its history into one of the biggest names in automotive tuning. Put simply, there is no other brand out there being quite so brave, or seemingly capturing the current tuning zeitgeist as this brand.

OUR COLLECTIONS

LB ★ Silhouette Works

While keeping with the proud tradition of Japan Grand Champion Series Silhouette Cars, Liberty Walk have revolutionised the way LB-Works cars are built! With the LB Silhouette Works GT range every panel on the car, with the exception of the roof and doors, will now be available with the new innovative wide-body full panel replacements.

It is Liberty Walk’s unique Japanese background and years of custom car building that has brought Liberty Walk to the point it is at today. As a new part of the LB-Works line-up, LB Silhouette Works GT will continue to define Liberty Walk for years to come.

LB ★ Performance

When you see Liberty Walk written, you immediately think of the widebody creations from Japan. Did you realise the LB Performance range offers an entire catalogue of front splitters, side skits, spoilers and more?

It is Liberty Walk’s unique Japanese background and years of custom car building that has brought Liberty Walk to the point it is at today. As a new part of the LB-Works line-up, LB Silhouette Works GT will continue to define Liberty Walk for years to come.

LB ★ Works

‘Works Fenders’ refer to a traditional aggressive full wide-body Liberty Walk kit. While honoring the original beauty of anexotic vehicle, Liberty Walk take their traditional Japanese custom roots of ‘Works Fenders’ and blend them with modernsupercars... The result: Liberty Walk have managed to take epic supercars and take them to the next unique level!

LB ★ Nation

Liberty Walk want everyone to be able to enjoy their products, from high-end supercar owners to your average car enthusiast! This is why they introduced the LB Nation series. They took the Suzuki Jimny and created a mini G-Class called the Mini-G. They took the Honda S660 and created the SSX-660R Mini NSX! With Liberty Walk, even small cars like these can become unique and fun mini-supercars!
